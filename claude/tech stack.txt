## ✅ **Summary**

* **Hyprland**: core Wayland compositor, window management, effects
* **Waybar + Eww**: bars, widgets, notifications, dashboards
* **Kvantum + Pywal**: theming Qt & GTK apps, dynamic colors
* **Fuzzel / Anyrun**: launchers
* **Kitty / WezTerm + Zsh + Starship + Neovim**: terminal ecosystem
* **Mako + swaylock-effects**: notifications & lockscreens
* Extras: swww (animated wallpapers), Glava (music visualizer), Conky/btop (monitoring)

This stack **replaces any DE (KDE/GNOME/XFCE)** and covers nearly everything in your checklist. Only bootloader, audio events, and niche KDE widgets require separate setup.
